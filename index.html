Tournament Structure

From state qualifiers to the grand finale

Monthly Qualifiers (16 States)

Each month, teams from 16 states compete in single-elimination knockout matches to determine the monthly champion.

Participating States

Lagos Ogun Osun Oyo Ondo Abuja Kaduna Kwara Anambra Enugu Rivers Abia Delta Edo Plateau Ekiti

Monthly Prizes

  • ₦2,000,000 Cash Prize
  • Team Jerseys for All Players
  • Gold Medals
  • Qualification for Lagos Grand Finale

Year-End Championship ("Kings of 5's")

The ultimate showdown featuring all 12 monthly winning teams competing for the grand prize and the title of "Kings of 5's".

Championship Format

The 12 monthly winners are divided into 4 groups of 3 teams each. After group stage matches, the top 2 teams from each group advance to the knockout stage.

Championship Prizes

  • ₦5,000,000 Grand Prize
  • Championship Trophy
  • 1-Year Brand Ambassadorship
  • Special Edition Championship Jerseys
  • Media Coverage and Recognition
