About Naija5Fest

Naija5Fest is Nigeria's premier 5-a-side football tournament combining grassroots competition with innovative sponsor integration. The tournament operates across 16 Nigerian states with monthly qualifiers leading to a prestigious year-end championship.

What makes Naija5Fest unique is our direct product-to-registration linkage, where teams register by purchasing sponsor products. This creates a powerful platform for brand exposure while developing grassroots football talent across Nigeria.

Naija5Fest today announced the complete schedule for its 2025 tournament series, featuring monthly qualifiers across 16 Nigerian states and the year-end championship in Lagos. Media Coverage Guidelines

Media Accreditation

Journalists and media representatives wishing to cover Naija5Fest events must apply for media accreditation at least 7 days before the event. Accreditation provides:

  • Access to media areas at tournament venues
  • Interview opportunities with players, coaches, and organizers
  • Access to high-resolution photos and videos
  • Press conference invitations

To apply for accreditation, please email media@naija5fest.com.ng with your name, media outlet, position, and the specific event(s) you wish to cover.

Brand Guidelines

When using Naija5Fest branding in your coverage, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Always use the correct spelling: "Naija5Fest" (one word, with the number 5)
  • Do not alter, distort, or change the colors of the official logos
  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "N" in the logo
  • When mentioning sponsors, use their full and correct names as provided in our materials

Interview Requests

For interview requests with Naija5Fest organizers, players, or sponsors, please contact our media relations team at least 48 hours in advance. We will do our best to accommodate all reasonable requests based on availability.
