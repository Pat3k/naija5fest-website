5. Tournament Registration and Participation

5.1 Eligibility

To participate in Naija5Fest tournaments, teams must:

  • Be based in one of the 16 participating states: Lagos, Ogun, Osun, Oyo, Ondo, Abuja, Kaduna, Kwara, Anambra, Enugu, Rivers, Abia, Delta, Edo, Plateau, Ekiti.
  • Have a minimum of 7 players and a maximum of 12 players per team.
  • All players must be at least 18 years of age at the time of registration.
  • Complete the registration process and pay all applicable fees.
  • Purchase the required sponsor products as specified for the tournament month.

5.2 Registration Process

Registration for Naija5Fest tournaments requires:

  • Completion of the online registration form with accurate team and captain information.
  • Payment of the tournament fee, which includes the purchase of the specified sponsor product and processing fee.
  • Acceptance of these terms and conditions and the tournament rules.
  • Verification of team eligibility by Naija5Fest staff.

5.3 Payment and Refunds

  • All payments are processed through secure payment gateways.
  • Registration fees are non-refundable except in cases where the tournament is canceled by Naija5Fest.
  • In the event of tournament cancellation, refunds will be processed within 30 days.
  • No refunds will be issued for team disqualification due to rule violations or failure to attend scheduled matches.

5.4 Tournament Rules

All teams must adhere to the official Naija5Fest Tournament Rules, which include:

  • 5-a-side format with rolling substitutions.
  • 20-minute matches (two 10-minute halves).
  • Single-elimination knockout format.
  • Fair play and sportsmanship requirements.
  • Prohibition of violent conduct, abusive language, or unsportsmanlike behavior.
  • Adherence to referee decisions.

The complete Tournament Rules document will be provided to all registered teams and is available upon request.

5.5 Prizes

Monthly tournament prizes include:

  • ₦2,000,000 cash prize for the winning team.
  • Team jerseys for all players on the winning team.
  • Gold medals for all players on the winning team.
  • Qualification for the Lagos Grand Finale.

Year-end championship prizes include:

  • ₦5,000,000 grand prize for the winning team.
  • Championship trophy.
  • 1-year brand ambassadorship for the winning team.
  • Special edition championship jerseys.
  • Media coverage and recognition.

All prizes are subject to applicable taxes and withholdings as required by Nigerian law.
